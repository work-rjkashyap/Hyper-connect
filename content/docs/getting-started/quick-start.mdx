---
title: Quick Start
description: Get up and running with Hyper Connect in minutes
---

Getting started with Hyper Connect is simple. Follow these steps to connect your devices and start sharing.

## 1. Connect to Local Network

Ensure all devices you want to connect are on the same Wi-Fi or Ethernet network.

## 2. Open Hyper Connect

Launch the application on at least two devices. You should see your device name appearing in the sidebar as it starts the discovery service.

## 3. Discover Devices

The app uses **Bonjour/mDNS** to find other instances of Hyper Connect on your network.

- **Active Devices** will appear in the "Available Devices" list.
- Click on a device name to initiate a connection.

## 4. Send a Message

Once connected, you can type a message in the chat box and hit Enter. The message will be delivered instantly to the other device.

## 5. Share a File

To share a file:

- **Drag and drop** a file into the chat window.
- Or click the **Plus (+)** icon to select a file from your system.
- The transfer will start immediately, and you can see the progress bar in real-time.

<Callout title="Pro Tip">
  You can send multiple files at once by selecting them in your file explorer before dragging them
  into the app.
</Callout>
